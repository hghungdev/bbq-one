Privacy Policy — BBQ-One

Last updated: April 2025

BBQ-One is a Chrome extension for personal note-taking, bookmark management, and dictionary lookup. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data required to provide the service:

  • Authentication credentials — email and password, used solely to log in via Supabase Auth.
  • Notes and folders — content you create, stored in your personal Supabase account.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identity information (name, address, phone number).
  • We do not collect browsing history or track visited pages.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect payment or financial information.

How Data Is Used

Your data is used exclusively to provide the core features of BBQ-One. It is never sold, shared, or transferred to any third party.

Data Storage & Security

Notes are stored in your personal Supabase project, protected by Row Level Security (RLS) — only you can access your own data. Notes inside Secure Folders are encrypted client-side (AES-GCM) before being sent to the server.

Third-Party Services

BBQ-One connects to Supabase (supabase.co) for authentication and data sync. No other third-party services receive your data.
